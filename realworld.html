Vt-shaped Students' Importance

In the podcast, one hears the importance of the Vt-shaped student model.

As Ben Anderson, the inventor of Themalwear, states is ready to take his product to the market; yet, as a beta tells him, his product has technical issues. The clothing that uses sensors to keep one's body temperature comfortable, could be tricked by emotions such as nerviousness. However, the testors states it is more important that the clothing needs to be cheaper for all people, whether it is fixed first or not.

Both are closed-minded, as the inventor only see money while the testor only sees the audience. Neither see how the product should be perfected first. With more worldly experience or more people to offer insight, each could have seen the underlying issue.

The point of the Vt-shaped student is to has these tools of worldy expereince, but alos this networking so people for referencing. With the model, one can do the best with inventing, and all facts of life.
