The VT-model's Impact

Three students--three first-hand takes of how the "V" part of the VT-shaped student has impacted Hokie students.

It starts with a kid with a dream. He visited Virginia Tech and found his love for the sports in Blacksburg. Now a student here, he started working to make a career out of that love. He not only found experience for his future career, through the Collegiate Times, but how to be an active part of a community through various activites and groups possible to join.

One then further hears in great detail on how outside the classroom Blacksburg helps give on-hand experience for another student. He says he will "leg up on the competetion" for future jobs.

Continuing on with jobs, one lastly a student not only experience for a further career, but just how far the VT-shaped student reaches. The student tutors refugees in Roanoke, helping students learn English. This not helps the students better adjust, but shows the power of the VT-shaped student outside Blacksburg.

Most schools offer the "T" model student, but Blacksburg is now offering the "VT" model. It may have started fairly recently, but it's impact looms large.
